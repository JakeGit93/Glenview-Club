Facilities

Main Bar

The Main Bar (Sports Bar) is the center of the club’s activities with a safe and friendly environment for everyone to enjoy.
The main bar is complimented with numerous large screens which screen live sports games creating a great place for people to relax in.
In the summer months you can enjoy the warmth from the balcony. The smoker’s area is popular with the smokers and non-smokers which then follow onto a BBQ area.

On Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights bar snacks and main meals can be ordered from the bar between 6pm and 8:30pm, We also have Sunday Brunch from 11am – 2pm.

Children are allowed in all areas of the main bar with the exception of the gaming area. There is a small area for children equipped with boards and some toys for them to enjoy.
